Welcome to GroundSchool

Flying is an experience like nothing else, and it’s also serious business. As pilots we need to know our stuff.

Whether you’re studying for your first exam, flight review, job interview, or you already have your license and know the importance of keeping current, our mission is to assist you by providing quality online flight training resources for New Zealand pilots.

The website is currently in closed beta but if you’d like to keep up to date with progress please enter your name and email, and as soon as it’s ready we’ll let you know.
